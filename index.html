Concept Pairs / 易混概念

Efficiency vs. Utilization

Efficiency uses effective capacity. Utilization uses design capacity.

效率分母是有效产能；利用率分母是设计产能。

Order Qualifiers vs. Order Winners

Qualifiers get a product considered. Winners make it selected.

资格要素让顾客考虑你；赢单要素让顾客选择你。

Process Layout vs. Product Layout

Process layout groups functions. Product layout follows sequence.

流程布局按功能分组；产品布局按加工顺序排列。
